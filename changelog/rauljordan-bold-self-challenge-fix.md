### Fixed
- Fixed honest-validator self-challenge bug in BoLD where a slow catchup goroutine could downgrade `latestAgreedAssertion`, causing the sync loop to skip the agreement check for the next on-chain assertion and trigger a doomed-to-revert `createLayerZeroEdge` transaction against one of the validator's own canonical assertions. `advanceChainPointer` now only moves the cursor forward when the new assertion is a direct child of the current `latestAgreedAssertion`. Added a defense-in-depth guard in `maybePostRivalAssertionAndChallenge` that bails before `HandleCorrectRival` when the computed "correct rival" has the same hash as the assertion being challenged.
